Potato Kugel 

            This crispy potato kugel is hands-down the best my family
            and many of my guests have ever tasted. It's easy to make 
            and fabulous served hot with sour cream. 
            It's also delicious added to a Jewish cholent (stew) and 
            cooked overnight.
  1. Prep Time:
    30 mins
  2. Cook Time:
    1 hr 30 mins
  3. Total Time:
    2 hrs
  1. Servings:
    24
  2. Prep Time:
    1 (9x13-inch) kugels
